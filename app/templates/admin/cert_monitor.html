Upload Certificate Chain —

Upload the signed certificate chain from your CA (PEM format). Include the server certificate and all intermediate CA certificates in order.

Accepted formats: .pem, .crt, .cer, X.509, PKCS#7 (.p7b). Paste the complete chain including -----BEGIN CERTIFICATE----- headers.
